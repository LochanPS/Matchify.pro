## For Players: How to Request a Refund

### Step 1: Navigate to My Registrations
1. Login to your account
2. Click on your profile menu
3. Select "My Registrations"

### Step 2: Find Your Registration
- You'll see all your tournament registrations
- Find the tournament you want to cancel
- Make sure the tournament hasn't started yet (cancellation not allowed after start)

### Step 3: Click "Cancel Registration"
- Look for the "Cancel Registration" button on the registration card
- Only available for `confirmed` or `pending` registrations

### Step 4: Fill the Cancellation Form
The modal will open with a form. You need to provide:

**Required Fields:**
- **Reason for Cancellation** (minimum 10 characters)
  - Be specific and detailed
  - Example: "I have an urgent family commitment and cannot attend the tournament"
  
- **UPI ID for Refund** (minimum 5 characters)
  - Your UPI ID where you want to receive the refund
  - Example: `yourname@paytm`, `9876543210@ybl`, etc.

**Optional Field:**
- **QR Code Screenshot**
  - Upload a screenshot of your UPI QR code
  - Helps organizer process refund faster
  - Max file size: 5MB
  - Only image files accepted

### Step 5: Submit Request
- Click "Submit Cancellation Request"
- You'll see a success message
- Your registration status will change to "Cancellation Requested"

### Step 6: Wait for Organizer Response
- The organizer will receive a notification
- They will review your request
- You'll receive a notification when they make a decision

### Step 7: Receive Refund
**If Approved:**
- You'll receive a notification that your refund was approved
- The organizer will manually send money to your UPI ID
- Once sent, they'll mark it as "Completed"
- You'll receive another notification confirming the refund
- Check your UPI account for the money

**If Rejected:**
- You'll receive a notification with the rejection reason
- Your registration will remain active
- You can still participate in the tournament

## For Organizers: How to Process Refunds

### Step 1: Receive Notification
- When a player requests cancellation, you'll receive a notification
- Notification type: "Cancellation Request Received"

### Step 2: View Cancellation Requests
1. Go to "Tournament Management"
2. Select your tournament
3. Click on the "Refund Requests" tab
4. You'll see all pending cancellation requests

### Step 3: Review Request Details
Each request shows:
- **Player Information**
  - Name, email, phone
  - Profile photo
  
- **Tournament Details**
  - Tournament name
  - Category name
  - Registration date
  
- **Refund Details**
  - Cancellation reason
  - UPI ID for refund
  - QR code (if provided)
  - Refund amount

### Step 4: Make a Decision

#### Option A: Approve Refund
1. Click "Approve Refund" button
2. Confirm the action
3. Registration status changes to "Cancelled"
4. Refund status changes to "Approved"
5. Player receives notification

**Next Steps After Approval:**
1. Open your UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, etc.)
2. Send money to the player's UPI ID
3. Amount: The refund amount shown
4. Once money is sent, return to the system
5. Click "Mark as Completed"
6. Player receives confirmation notification

#### Option B: Reject Refund
1. Click "Reject Refund" button
2. A modal will open asking for a reason
3. Provide a clear reason (minimum 5 characters)
   - Example: "Tournament is starting in 2 days, cannot process refund at this time"
4. Submit rejection
5. Registration status returns to "Confirmed"
6. Refund status changes to "Rejected"
7. Player receives notification with your reason

### Step 5: Track Refund Status
You can see the status of all refunds:
- **Pending** - Waiting for your decision
- **Approved** - Approved but money not sent yet
- **Completed** - Money sent and marked complete
- **Rejected** - Request was rejected

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Can I cancel after the tournament has started?
**A:** No, cancellation is only allowed before the tournament start date.

### Q: How long does it take to get my refund?
**A:** It depends on the organizer. Once approved, they manually send the money via UPI. Most refunds are processed within 24-48 hours.

### Q: What if I don't receive the refund after approval?
**A:** You can use the "Report Issue" button to notify the organizer. They will receive an alert.

### Q: Can I cancel a cancellation request?
**A:** No, once submitted, you need to wait for the organizer's decision. If rejected, your registration remains active.

### Q: Is the QR code mandatory?
**A:** No, it's optional. But uploading it helps the organizer process your refund faster.

### Q: What happens to my registration after cancellation?
**A:** Your registration is marked as "Cancelled" and you won't be able to participate in the tournament. The spot becomes available for others.

### Q: Can I re-register after cancellation?
**A:** Yes, if the tournament is still accepting registrations, you can register again (but you'll need to pay the entry fee again).

### Q: As an organizer, do I have to approve all refund requests?
**A:** No, you can reject requests if you have valid reasons (e.g., tournament starting soon, administrative costs, etc.). Just provide a clear reason.

### Q: What if I accidentally reject a refund request?
**A:** Contact the player directly to resolve the issue. The system doesn't allow reversing decisions, but you can manually process the refund outside the system if needed.
