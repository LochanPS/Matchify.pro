/scoring/:matchId - Score match (umpire/organizer)

===========================================
WEBSOCKET EVENTS
===========================================

Client Emits:
- join-tournament (tournamentId)
- leave-tournament (tournamentId)

Client Receives:
- tournament-match-update
  * action: 'started' | 'score-updated' | 'completed'
  * matchId: Match ID
  * status: Current status
  * score: Score data
  * winner: Winner data (if completed)
