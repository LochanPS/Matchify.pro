# ✅ Payment System: 30% + 65% + 5%

## Quick Summary

The payment system now works as follows:

### Payment Split
- **30%** → Paid to organizer BEFORE tournament starts
- **65%** → Paid to organizer AFTER tournament completes  
- **5%** → You keep as platform fee

**Total: 100%**

## How It Works

1. **Players register** and pay to your admin QR code
2. **You approve** their payments in the admin panel
3. **System calculates** automatically:
   - 30% for first payout
   - 65% for second payout
   - 5% platform fee (you keep)
4. **Before tournament**: You pay organizer 30% using their QR code
5. **After tournament**: You pay organizer 65% using their QR code
6. **Platform fee**: Automatically stays with you (5%)

- **Tournament Payments Page**: Shows all tournaments with payment breakdown
- **Organizer Payouts Page**: Shows pending payouts with organizer QR codes
- Both pages show "First 30%" and "Remaining 65%" labels
