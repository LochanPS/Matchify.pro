## 7. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY

### 7.1 Data Location
**All data is stored in India:**
- Database: PostgreSQL on Render (India region)
- Images: Cloudinary (Asia region)
- Backups: India-based servers
- Compliant with DPDP Act 2023 data localization requirements

### 7.2 Security Measures

**Technical Security:**
- HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
- Bcrypt password hashing (irreversible)
- Database encryption at rest
- Regular security updates
- Firewall protection
- DDoS protection
- Rate limiting to prevent brute force attacks

**Access Controls:**
- Role-based access control
- Admin actions logged in audit trail
- Two-factor authentication (for admins)
- Session timeout after inactivity
- IP-based access restrictions

**Organizational Security:**
- Employee confidentiality agreements
- Limited access to personal data
- Regular security training
- Incident response plan
- Data breach insurance

### 7.3 Payment Security
- We do NOT store credit/debit card details
- Payment screenshots encrypted
- UPI IDs masked in logs
- Transaction data encrypted
- PCI-DSS compliant practices (where applicable)

### 7.4 Security Limitations
**Despite our efforts, no system is 100% secure:**
- You are responsible for protecting your password
- Do not share your account credentials
- Log out after using shared devices
- Report suspicious activity immediately

## 9. YOUR RIGHTS (DPDP ACT 2023)

Under the Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023, you have the following rights:

### 9.1 Right to Access
- Request a copy of your personal data
- Receive data in a readable format
- Free of charge (first request)
- Response within 30 days

**How to exercise:** Email dpo@matchify.pro with subject "Data Access Request"

### 9.2 Right to Correction
- Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
- Update your profile information
- Free of charge
- Changes effective immediately

**How to exercise:** Update in your profile settings or email dpo@matchify.pro

### 9.3 Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten)
- Request deletion of your personal data
- Account will be permanently deleted
- Some data retained for legal compliance
- Process completed within 30 days

**How to exercise:** Email dpo@matchify.pro with subject "Account Deletion Request"

**Exceptions:** We may retain data if:
- Required by law (tax records)
- Needed for legal proceedings
- Necessary to prevent fraud
- Part of anonymized analytics

### 9.4 Right to Data Portability
- Receive your data in machine-readable format (JSON/CSV)
- Transfer data to another service
- Free of charge
- Provided within 30 days

**How to exercise:** Email dpo@matchify.pro with subject "Data Portability Request"

### 9.5 Right to Withdraw Consent
- Withdraw consent for data processing
- May limit Platform functionality
- Does not affect past processing
- Effective immediately

**How to exercise:** Email dpo@matchify.pro or adjust settings in your account

### 9.6 Right to Nominate
- Nominate someone to exercise your rights after death
- Nominee can access or delete your data
- Must provide legal documentation

**How to exercise:** Email dpo@matchify.pro with nominee details

### 9.7 Right to Grievance Redressal
- File complaint with our Grievance Officer
- Response within 24 hours
- Resolution within 15 days
- Escalate to Data Protection Board if unsatisfied

**How to exercise:** Email grievance@matchify.pro
