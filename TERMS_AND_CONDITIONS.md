### 5.1 Payment Structure
- **Entry Fees:** Set by Tournament Organizers
- **Platform Fee:** 5% of entry fee (included in total)
- **Payment Method:** UPI transfer to admin account
- **Payment Verification:** Manual verification by admin
- **Payment Proof:** Screenshot upload required

**Payment Flow:**
1. Player pays 100% entry fee to Matchify.pro admin UPI account
2. Player uploads payment screenshot
3. Admin verifies payment within 24-48 hours
4. Upon approval, registration is confirmed
5. Matchify.pro keeps 5% platform fee
6. Matchify.pro pays Organizer 95% in 2 installments:
   - **30% BEFORE tournament starts**
   - **65% AFTER tournament completes**

### 5.2 Payment Process
1. Player selects tournament and categories
2. Total amount calculated (entry fee inclusive of 5% platform fee)
3. Player pays to Matchify.pro admin UPI account
4. Player uploads payment screenshot
5. Admin verifies payment within 24-48 hours
6. Upon approval, registration is confirmed
7. Matchify.pro pays Organizer their share (95%)

### 5.3 Payment Distribution
- **To Matchify.pro:** 5% platform fee (retained immediately)
- **To Organizer:** 95% of entry fee (paid in 2 installments)
  - **First Payment (30%):** Paid BEFORE tournament starts
  - **Second Payment (65%):** Paid AFTER tournament completes

**Example:** If entry fee is ₹1,000
- Player pays: ₹1,000 to Matchify.pro admin
- Platform keeps: ₹50 (5%)
- Organizer receives total: ₹950 (95%)
  - Before tournament: ₹300 (30% of ₹1,000)
  - After tournament: ₹650 (65% of ₹1,000)
