## 📱 Mobile-First Design Principles Applied

### Spacing Hierarchy
- **Container**: `p-4` or `p-5` (never `p-6` or `p-8`)
- **Cards**: `p-4` (never `p-5` or `p-6`)
- **Modals**: `p-5` for content (compact and centered)
- **Buttons**: `py-2.5` or `py-3` (never `py-3.5` or `py-4`)
- **Section gaps**: `gap-3` to `gap-5` (never `gap-6` or `gap-8`)

### Typography Hierarchy
- **Page titles**: `text-xl md:text-2xl font-black`
- **Section headers**: `text-base font-bold`
- **Modal titles**: `text-lg font-black`
- **Labels**: `text-sm font-bold`
- **Body text**: `text-sm`
- **Helper text**: `text-xs`

### Component Sizes
- **Large icons**: `w-10 h-10` with `h-5 w-5` icon
- **Medium icons**: `w-8 h-8` with `h-4 w-4` icon
- **Buttons**: `px-4` or `px-5`, `py-2.5` or `py-3`
- **Badges**: `px-2 py-0.5` for compact, `px-3 py-1` for normal
- **Modals**: `max-w-sm` (not `max-w-md` or `max-w-lg`)

### Border Radius
- **Cards**: `rounded-xl` (not `rounded-2xl`)
- **Buttons**: `rounded-xl` or `rounded-lg`
- **Badges**: `rounded-full` or `rounded-lg`
- **Modals**: `rounded-2xl` (exception for top-level containers)
